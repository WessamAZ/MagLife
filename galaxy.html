Galaxy

Real Time Mobile Network Monitoring Solution.

What is Galaxy?

Galaxy is a real-time monitoring system for mobile network aiming to enhance the Quality of Experience (QoE) of mobile subscribers and to increase the revenue of mobile operators. These legitimate objectives can be met together by adapting the provided services to the real need of customers. The adaptation process needs a deep knowledge about the expectations of subscribers and the Quality of Service (QoS) of the deployed mobile network in real time.
With Galaxy, the mobile operator knows better the behavior of each subscriber such as his consumption habits with respect to time, geographic location and his social network. This gives a great insight about the customer profile and his interests. This can be achieved at the individual level as well as at a large scale (categorical or regional) such as for roamers or users in a specific geographic location. A special care of VIP subscribers can be provided by a dedicated monitoring filters. Real-time promotion marketing offers based on the subscriber's profile, location, or consumption behavior can be implemented such as dynamic offering and tariffing.
Galaxy keeps an eye open on all transactions through the mobile network; helping to monitor any glitch or problem that may arise in mobile sites or the core network elements. Obviously, this allows for an immediate action to avoid revenue loss and to ensure a sustained high QoS for various services. As a mobile operator, you can respond in real time to any user complaint; you know the load distribution among different cells which help to adapt your network planning and marketing tactics in a dynamic way. Consequently, less churn and higher customer satisfaction are attainable.
Collected information by Galaxy are available in real time and are recorded for historical analysis in the form of Call Data Registers (CDRs), Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), Alarms, and Raw Data (RD) for finer technical diagnostics when necessary. Real time streaming is optionally supported for real-time dynamic offering and third party applications.
Galaxy is built using efficient software which has low requirements on hardware. In the same time, Galaxy provides an easy to use graphical user interface through a wide range of visualization tools including dashboards, geographic maps, and reports.

Architecture

Galaxy collects the raw data from multiple interfaces in the mobile core network using multiple probes. Galaxy probes capture the network packets and dissect them in order to extract the useful information for monitoring purposes. In addition, Galaxy probes correlate different packets related to the same session or dialogue, and group them in a single register called CDR.

CDRs contain different relevant attributes that reflects the needed information to be used at the application layer. Galaxy probes send collected transactions in real time to the Galaxy mediation servers. The mediation servers have an important role in global correlation of different transactions, KPIs and alarms generation, and data enrichment by converting numerical values to a human readable form which reflects the meaning of the numerical codes such as the "Country Name" or the "Operator Name" instead of the numerical codes MCC or MNC.

Real-time and history monitoring

Galaxy uses Elastic Search as history database and Kibana as Graphic User Interface (GUI). You can explore generated CDRs up to the current instant, with rich filtering and aggregation capabilities. Data analysis has never been easier and can be applied on the fly to extract hidden information.
Galaxy can process and generate more than one Billion CDRs per day and can be easily scaled up to accommodate your business growth. Historical CDrs data are available back to three months ago and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) back to three years ago.

Customer Quality of Experience

Subscriber experience with the different provided services such frequency of use, drop calls or rejected calls with respect to geographic location can be monitored to provide special assistance in order to increase the customer satisfaction with the help of promotion offers that help to reduce churn rate and attract new subscribers. Moreover, Galaxy can provide a real-time data stream for third party applications for analytics, Fault Management System (FMS), or promotion such as Real-Time Dynamic Offering System (RTDOS) aiming to interact with subscribers in real-time based on predefined rules.

Real-time Network Quality of Service

With the help of various visualization capabilities of Galaxy, the performance of the whole mobile network can be monitored in real-time using dashboards. For example, you can use geo-location maps for sites, split bars for MSCs, ..etc.

Real-time Fault detection with alarms

To provide high QoS and to reduce the loss due to the out-of-service, the mobile operator must handle faults and incidents in the network as fast as possible. Galaxy provide KPIs per site/network elements that reflect performance and incidents alarms to alert for any fault in the mobile network.

Extracting and monitoring of VIP customers

Based on the total average consumption or other criterion, you can recognize you VIP subscribers. These subscribers are worth of special care in order to avoid their churn. SIM box detection and service center can be recognized by a simple aggregation filer.

Hardware requirements

Galaxy uses commodity and available hardware in its design. The minimum requirements for the hardware are as follows:

One probe at each location of core network elements.
One mediation server.
Three ES cluster servers.

The actual sizing of the system depends on the current data flow rate and the expansion plan of the operator. We can provide the most suitable sizing after contacts.
